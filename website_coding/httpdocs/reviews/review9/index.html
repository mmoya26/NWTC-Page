Website Review of Facebook

I used Facebook's page for my example of a form, in this case, it is their register form, where users can sign up to have an account on Facebook. I believe that the method for this form is to "post" it to their database so they can save our information so we can, later on, log into Facebook. They have a couple different types of input's, one for email or phone number, a couple of radio buttons, and also drop-down menus too.

Overall the website it's pretty clean I mean it's Facebook, they are a big company and they put a lot thought on their design.
