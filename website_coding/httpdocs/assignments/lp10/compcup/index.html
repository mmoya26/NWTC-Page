Competitive Cup Stacking

In this new Olympic sport, athletes will compete in two categories.

Tallest Stacked Cup Monument

This is a team event where teams of at least 2, but no more than 5 athletes will join forces to build the tallest stacked cup monument. Standard-issued plastic cups of varied colors will be provided. No additional tools, including ladders, can be used. The structure must stand without any additional support for 3 minutes to be considered as a medal winning monument.

Fastest Stack

Individuals will be competing in a timed cup stacking event. All individuals are competing in one bracket. Athletes will be given 144 standard-issues plastic cups of varied colors to use. The top 3 athletes to construct and de-construct the structure using all plastic cups without issue will be awarded medals.
